COVID-19 Update: Due to the extended public health crisis being caused by COVID-19, Community Forge is currently closed. Through our partners, Opening Doors for Youth and Families and Confluence Catering, we will be serving free, take-away breakfast, lunch, and dinner to at-need families, which can be picked up at our loading dock near the playground from 9AM-1PM or 3-7PM. Currently both programs operate Monday-Friday, but return here for updates. We are also starting an emergency fund to support our entrepreneurs and families. If you are a Wilkinsburg community member, Community Forge program participant, or tenant struggling due to the COVID crisis, please write us at hello@forge.community to get an application. If you are considering donating to our fund, scroll down to donate below. If you are looking for emergency business resources due to COVID-19, see this resource guide kindly provided by our friends at Riverside Center for Innovation.
Please stay safe and healthy and we'll update this page once we re-open to the public.
