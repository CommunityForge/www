Community Forge Events

Community Forge is proud to host a wide range of public events, many of which are free or reduced price for Wilkinsburg residents. Past events run by community organizations and Community Forge have included: Mac n’ Cheese cook-off, CF Annual BBQ and SunFest celebration of music and art, Toy Drive, community health documentaries, and much more. Check out our listings below for upcoming events and programs.

Booking Your Event

Community Forge’s converted school rooms have been host to bookings big and small, from music festivals to workshops, community meetings to film screenings, conference calls to birthday parties.

We would love to host your next event or hourly booking! Community Forge has a variety of spaces to meet your needs. Please contact us at hello@forge.community, via our contact form, or by phone (412-501-2055) to schedule a tour and learn more. See below for pricing and more information.
